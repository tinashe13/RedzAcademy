Our Mission

The Redz Hockey Academy's mission is "to provide youth hockey players with the best quality methodology that allows them to be the top quality hockey players that are competitive at an international level and to help them reach their goals."

Let's Introduce the Academy

The Redz Hockey Academy was launched in February 2019 with the aim of promoting the growth of hockey in Zimbabwe and enhancing the skills of local players to better meet the International Standard. The Redz Hockey Academy aims to create World Class hockey players and at the same time develop the sport of hockey by introducing it in areas that have never experienced this sport especially the rural and high-density area schools.

Redz Hockey Academy offers training for both hockey athletes and coaches across Zimbabwe for beginners to elite levels and it offers training to athletes from 6-21 years of age.

The Academy aims to provide your child with the adequate tools and skills to develop their hockey as we aim to provide players with the necessary coaching to enhance their skills and growth as a hockey player to meet International Standards.

We offer group coaching, skills development training and small sided games during our practice sessions. We also offer individual video analysis and all Members get an end of month report highlighting their progress and areas to improve on.

Redz Hockey Academy uses St Johns College Astro turf for all sessions. We run 8 sessions or more per month and our program runs throughout the year. The Academy will also host Super Sunday Skills Clinics at schools outside Harare mainly the boarding schools to also help those players that are not able to attend our Academy sessions because they are at school.
