Elite Clinics

-

Redz Hockey Academy provides an Elite Hockey Clinic annually. The Clinic aims to target all top hockey junior players across Zimbabwe and that includes all National junior players and young and upcoming players. The Clinic aims to assist all the players with advanced skills training and grow their hockey knowledge to prepare them for the hockey season that starts in May. This Clinic also include classroom video sessions.

SMALL GROUP/PRIVATE LESSONS

-

Redz Hockey Academy also offers small group and private one on one sessions. These sessions can be for 30/60 minutes and typically take place after school or during the holidays. Small group and private sessions provide an excellent opportunity for the Redz Hockey Academy coaches to analyze each players needs and provide productive feedback on what they need to improve. Please contact for more information

DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS

-

Redz Hockey Academy will also be introducing hockey in rural and high density areas through its Grassroots Development program. The program will look to introduce hockey in areas that have never experience hockey before and also assist the schools and players financially through the Redz Hockey Foundation. We have already worked with Waruka Academy and Camryn Litjens in introducing hockey in Murewa.
