About the Academy

Redz Hockey Academy offers training for both hockey athletes and coaches across Zimbabwe for beginners to elite levels and it offers training to athletes from 6-21 years of age. The Academy aims to provide your child with the adequate tools and skills to develop their hockey as we aim to provide players with the necessary coaching to enhance their skills and growth as a hockey player to meet International Standards.
