Kanyiwe Tafuma

Director of Coaching

Player Profile

Kanyiwe is the ‘Director of Coaching’ for the Academy.
He is a former National hockey player for field hockey and is currently the Vice-Captain of the Zimbabwe Men’s Indoor Hockey team.
He was the former captain of the Hippo Hockey Club and is a Federation International Hockey Level 2 Certified coach.
He also has an‘Africa Hockey Level 1 Certified Coach. He is currently the ‘Assistant Coach’ for the Zimbabwe Senior Men’s Hockey team
He is also the Head of the Hockey Association of Zimbabwe Coaching and Development Committee.
He has coached at Eaglesvale and Arundel and currently coaches at St John’s College. Currently Playing for Hockey Club team in Harare
